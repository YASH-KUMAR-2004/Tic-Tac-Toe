Game Rules

  • Click a square to place your symbol (X or O).
  • Three in a row (horizontal, vertical, diagonal) wins.
  • Use the toggle to switch between Player vs Player or Computer.
  • The game will reset after a win or a draw.

Game Instructions

This is a 3x3 grid game. You can play against a friend or the computer. Use the button above to toggle modes. Make your move and see if you can win!

Computer moves are random. The game will notify you of a winner or a draw with a popup and reset automatically.
